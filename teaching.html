teaching

I served on the course staff of EECS 16B (the second lower-division electrical engineering course at UC Berkeley) in various positions from August 2017 to August 2020, winning an Outstanding Graduate Student Instructor award in 2020 for my contributions to the course.

Detailed Teaching History

Responsibilities for each role also include responsibilities for all roles listed above it in the table.

Semester Role Responsibilities
Fall 2017 Academic Intern Assist students one-on-one during lab sections
Spring 2018 Lab TA Give lab lectures, lead sections, update labs, grade labs, answer questions on Piazza, write exam questions, grade exams
Fall 2018
Spring 2019 Lab Content TA* Write lab notes (lab manual), design new labs, write homework questions
Fall 2019
Spring 2020 Head Lab TA Administrate lab portion of course: assign students to sections and groups, schedule TAs and AIs, assign final lab grades, train AIs, post content and admin communications on Piazza and course website, restructure lab logistics for remote instruction
Summer 2020
* I originated this role and wrote the first edition of the manual.
